How Our Collective Funds Work

Our collective investment structures pool capital from multiple investors to create larger, more efficient investment vehicles. This provides access to opportunities typically only available to institutional investors.

The typical investment lifecycle begins with capital commitments from our investor base, followed by a 3-6 month investment period where we deploy capital into carefully vetted opportunities. Our holding periods range from 3-7 years depending on the asset class and strategy, with clear exit protocols to maximize returns.

Investment Process

  • Initial capital commitment from investors with clear target allocations
  • Professional due diligence and asset acquisition using our 120-point checklist
  • Active asset management and value enhancement through operational improvements
  • Ongoing monitoring and performance reporting with quarterly deep dives
  • Strategic exits and capital returns timed to market conditions
  • Post-investment review and performance analysis for continuous improvement
